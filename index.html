About

Custom Manufacturer Of

Control Panel Facias, Instrument Panel Facias, Front Panels, Back Panels, Enclosures, Membrane Overlays, Architectual Signs & Plaques, Nameplates, Labels Dials & Scales for the Electronic, Mechanical & Architectual Sectors. Subcontract CNC sheet metal punching, folding & light fabrication.

Our Service

To produce from customers drawings, samples or through joint development, quality parts that enhance our customers products, in small to medium quantities & manufactured to tight deadlines.

Company Profile

John Beaumont Ltd. is an independent company, established in 1945 and staffed by experienced personnel, the majority whom have been with the company many years. We have a policy of continuous improvement & development, our commitment to this is shown in our investment in equipment, ranging from CAD/CAM & graphics design computers, CNC punching machines, CNC routers & CNC press brake, to screen printing machines, engraving machines, & finishing equipment.

Trade Services

Sign & Engraving Trade Service

We supply various trade customers with the following. Can we help you? Please contact us for more details, quotations dealt with promptly. All enquiries treated in strictest confidence.

Anodised Aluminium Sheets

We carry stocks of anodising quality aluminium sheet which we can supply satin anodised either sealed, or unsealed suitable for printing with Aluprint dyes. Our standard size is 1000mm. x 660mm.Thickness from .5mm to 3mm Other sizes can be supplied, please enquire.

Anodising Service

We can anodise silver, black & various colours. Ideal for aluminium panels that have been engraved, holes cut, countersunk, bevelled edges etc. where un-anodised areas must not show.

Aluprint Printing Service

Aluprint dyes screen printed onto unsealed anodised aluminium, then sealed, creates a highly durable & practical product. Ideal for machine nameplates, serial plates, control panel facias etc. We can work from computer generated artwork supplied either on disc, email attachment or drawing.

Etched Nameplates, Plaques, Signs & Facias

Chemical etching on brass & stainless steel. Using computer generated artwork we can faithfully reproduce your graphics then paint fill. Ideal for plaques with complex logos & crests, also volume work such as machine nameplates.

Punched Metal Blanks & Panels

Using our CNC punching machines we can punch out sheet metal components from 'dog' tags to control panel facias very efficiently, especially so in quantity.

Control Panel Fascias

Control & Instrument Panel Fascias

Control Panel Fascias, Instrument Panel Fascias & backpanels manufactured in anodised & aluchromed aluminium, stainless steel, painted steel & aluminium, acrylic, polycarbonate, PVC, Formica, etc.

With graphics applied by screen printing, chemical / photo engraving, machine engraving or Aluprinting.

Our artwork department can design suitable graphics or use customers designs & files.

Metal facias can be bent, folded, & fitted with threaded studs, bushes & stand-offs.

Our Aluprinted aluminium facias are anodised in house after cutting, punching,

bending & surface finishing, therefore all edges, countersunk holes & bevels etc. are anodised.

Anodising is a very hard durable coating for aluminium which can be permanently dyed with

a range of colours to produce multi-coloured graphics on silver or coloured backgrounds.

Stainless steel facias up to 3mm thick can be manufactured using our Trumpf CNC punching machines then engraved, etched or screen printed as required.

Membrane overlays

Membrane overlays are manufactured to customers requirements using only top quality materials, they can incorporate clear window areas & can be supplied mounted on backpanels complete with studs & stand-offs ready to mount your circuit boards.

Nameplates

Nameplates, plaques & serial no. plates manufactured to customers requirements by machine engraving, chemical etching, Aluprinting, & screen printing onto stainless steel, anodised aluminium, brass & various plastics. We can advise on the best methods for your application.

Enclosures & Chassis

Custom built sheet metal boxes, instrument cases & chassis can be manufactured in steel, zintec, stainless steel & aluminium, fitted with studs, bushes & stand-offs & finished in stove enamel, zinc plating, or silver or coloured anodising.

Screen Printing

Screen printing is a versatile printing system capable of printing a wide variety of inks & dyes ranging from epoxy two pack inks to Aluprint dyes (which can be permanently sealed into the surface of anodised aluminium).

As well as printing onto our own products we can print onto customers own items such as keyboard cases, boxes,extrusions etc. Our artwork department can produce suitable graphics or use customers designs & files

Pad Printing

Pad printing is a versatile printing system capable of printing a variety of inks on to shaped components and parts made in a wide variety of materials.

Pad printing is ideal for printing single or multicoloured graphics onto 3 dimensioned small parts that cannot be screen printed. Pad printing has the ability to print onto almost any surface, uneven & textured including plastic, wood, leather, glass & many others. Typical components that are pad printed are numerous, from injection molded electrical components, cases & fascias to promotional items such as pens, rubbers & balls.

Maximum print area: 80mm diameter.

Our artwork department can produce suitable graphics or use customers designs & files.

Industrial Engraving

Machine engraving & photo/chemical engraving.

Our machine engraving department is equipped with both computer controlled & pantograph industrial engraver/routers which are used to engrave anything from small formica tags to stainless steel cabinet doors, some over 1 metre high.

Sheet Metal Work

Our sheet metal department is well equipped & our skilled staff are used to producing precision components to our customers requirements.

We can offer a complete package from concept through design & prototyping, to production. Typical components that we produce include - Enclosures, chassis, facias, & backplates for the electronics industries, Perforated sheets, grills & blankings.
